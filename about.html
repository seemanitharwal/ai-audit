About

An engineer who audits like an engineer

AI Growth Audit exists because most "AI consulting" is vague. This isn't. Every recommendation comes from someone who has actually built the systems being recommended.

Who's Behind This

Founder & AI Automation Specialist

I spent the first part of my career writing production software — backend systems, APIs, and data pipelines for real businesses. Over the last few years, that shifted into applied AI: chatbots, WhatsApp automation, retrieval-augmented knowledge bases, and workflow automation using tools like LangChain, LangGraph, and n8n.

AI Growth Audit is the product of a simple observation: most SMBs don't need more software pitched at them — they need someone to tell them, clearly and specifically, where AI will actually move the needle for their business. So that's what the audit does. No jargon, no inflated promises, just a score, a report, and a plan.

If you decide you want help implementing what the audit finds, that's a natural next step — but it's never the point of the audit itself.
