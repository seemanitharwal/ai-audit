Executive Summary

This brand runs a solid Shopify storefront with decent product content, but is leaving significant revenue on the table in customer support and lead capture. Response times average 6+ hours on Instagram DMs and WhatsApp, and roughly 1 in 3 website visitors who ask a product question never receive an answer. Top 5 Opportunities
1
WhatsApp AI support agent

Currently zero automation on WhatsApp despite it being the #1 customer contact channel. A first-response bot could cut average reply time from 6 hours to under 1 minute.

2
Website chatbot for product questions

An estimated 32% of size/fit/shipping questions go unanswered before the visitor leaves. A trained chatbot could recover a meaningful share of these as sales.

3
Automated lead follow-up sequence

Email capture exists but there's no follow-up after signup. A simple automated nurture sequence is a fast, low-cost win.

4
AI search / GEO optimization

Product pages aren't structured for AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity). Low effort, growing channel.

5
Order-status automation

"Where is my order" questions make up an estimated 40% of support volume — fully automatable.
